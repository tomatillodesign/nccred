North Carolina Commission on Racial & Ethnic Disparities in the Criminal Justice System

The North Carolina Commission on Racial & Ethnic Disparities in the Criminal Justice System (NC CRED) worked for 10 years in the pursuit of innovative, empirically-based solutions to reform the criminal justice system.

The Commission was formally dissolved in February 2025.

Thank you for your interest in our work.
